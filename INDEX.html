OUR SERVICES

OUR SERVICES

The company offers the following services to our clients and customer.

  • Sourcing and Procurement

    • Fruit distributors establish relationships with farms and producers both locally and internationally to source a wide variety of fruits.

  • Logistics and Transportation

    • Ensuring fruits are transported in temperature-controlled environments to preserve freshness, especially for perishable fruits.
    Distributing fruits directly to retailers, grocery stores, or restaurants, ensuring timely delivery.

  • Quality Control

    • Ensuring that the fruits meet quality standards before distribution.
    This includes size, ripeness, appearance, and flavor testing.

  • Direct-to-Consumer Sales

    • Distributing offer direct-to-consumer services through online platforms, allowing customers
    to order fresh fruit directly from the distributor.

  • Consulting Services

    • Fruit Variety Selection- Helping retailers and restaurants choose the right types of
    fruits based on their customer preferences, seasons, and trends.
    Providing information on fruit consumption trends, helping businesses adapt to changing consumer demands.

  • Training and Education

    • Providing training to retailers' staff on handling, displaying, and preserving fruits to ensure they remain in good condition.
